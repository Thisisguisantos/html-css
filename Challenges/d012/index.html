Modern Cordel

By Milton Duarte

I'm getting tired
of such technology
Only speak by email
short and cold message
Twitter and Facebook
before i expire
I will say everything in poetry.

It's not like it used to be
It's all abbreviated
"You" has only one letter
The "Y" and "O" has been crossed out
to declare love
Just put a flower
And a heart drawn.

Arroba now doesn't weigh
Is part of an address
End point is not used
Or go to the beginning
now it's .com
If the site is very good
He is worth a high price.

To make the language worse
The emoticon is a risk
There's a symbol for everything
Semicolon and an asterisk
a kiss means
to understand how it is
Decipher this scribble.

I miss the cards
Written by hand
Sent in June
only arrived in summer
But I missed you
It was real text
In the lines of the heart.

Now I write and send
arriving at the same time
But when will I talk
the person left
opened another app
The world was captive
From the technology of now.

Fortunately, to pray
don't need internet
God listens to everyone
If you want, take this test
Two points are two knees
Your lips are appliances
Let God interpret.
